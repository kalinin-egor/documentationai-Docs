---
title: Cannot Connect to Rented GPU
description: How to fix connection issues with your GPUiq instance.
---

## Problem

- Server not responding after rental
- SSH or remote desktop times out

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check rental status">
    ````bash
    Visit your GPUiq dashboard. Make sure your GPU is 'Active'.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify SSH details">
    ````bash
    Use the exact SSH IP and port shown under your rental details.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart instance">
    ````bash
    Click 'Restart' in the dashboard to resolve common setup issues.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
