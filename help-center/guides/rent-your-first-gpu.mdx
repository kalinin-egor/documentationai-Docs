## Quick Start

Rent a powerful GPU in a few steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    ````
    Go to gpuiq.com and log in to your account.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a GPU">
    ````
    Select your desired GPU model (NVIDIA, AMD, etc.) and location.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure rental">
    ````
    Set duration, storage, and features like snapshots or auto-switch.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start instance">
    ````
    Click 'Rent Now'. Access details will appear instantly in your dashboard.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
