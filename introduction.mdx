## Overview

GPUniq provides on-demand GPU rentals for compute-intensive workloads like AI training, machine learning inference, 3D rendering, and cryptocurrency mining. You access high-performance NVIDIA GPUs at affordable prices, with up to 70% cost savings compared to traditional cloud providers. Features like automatic switching between instances and snapshot backups make your workflows seamless and reliable.

Scale your projects without upfront hardware investments. Whether you run large language models or render complex scenes, GPUiq delivers the power you need, when you need it.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus" title-type="p">
    Sign up at `https://dashboard.gpuiq.com/register` with your email.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose GPU" icon="settings" title-type="p">
    Select from A100, RTX 4090, or custom configurations based on your needs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Deploy Instance" icon="rocket" title-type="p">
    Launch with one click. Use SSH or our API for access.

    ```bash
    curl -X POST https://api.gpuiq.com/v1/instances \
      -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
      -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
      -d '{
        "gpu_type": "A100",
        "vcpus": 8,
        "memory_gb": 40
      }'
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run Your Workload" icon="play-circle" title-type="p">
    Upload data, execute scripts, and scale as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
