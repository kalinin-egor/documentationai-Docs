## Overview

GPUniq provides on-demand GPU rentals for compute-intensive workloads like AI training, machine learning inference, 3D rendering, and cryptocurrency mining. You access high-performance NVIDIA GPUs at affordable prices, with up to 70% cost savings compared to traditional cloud providers. Our marketplace connects you directly with GPU owners worldwide, ensuring competitive pricing and instant availability.

Scale your projects without upfront hardware investments. Whether you run large language models or render complex scenes, GPUniq delivers the power you need, when you need it.

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus" title-type="p">
    Sign up at [gpuniq.com](https://gpuniq.com) with your email or Google account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Balance" icon="wallet" title-type="p">
    Deposit funds using USDT (TRC20) or YooKassa for Russian users.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse Marketplace" icon="search" title-type="p">
    Filter GPUs by model, VRAM, location, and hourly price. View real-time availability.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Rent & Connect" icon="rocket" title-type="p">
    Click "Rent" on your chosen GPU. Get instant SSH access credentials.

    ```bash
    ssh root@<host> -p <port>
    # Password provided in dashboard
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## API Access

Automate your GPU rentals with our REST API.

```bash
curl -X GET https://api.gpuniq.com/v1/marketplace/list \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"
```
