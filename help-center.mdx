## Common Topics

### Payments & Billing

- **How do I add funds?** Go to Balance â†’ Deposit. Choose USDT (TRC20) or YooKassa.
- **When am I charged?** Billing starts immediately when you rent. Charges are deducted in real-time.
- **Can I get a refund?** Contact support for billing issues. Unused balance can be withdrawn.

### GPU Rentals

- **How do I connect?** After renting, use the SSH credentials shown in your dashboard.
- **Why can't I connect?** Check that the instance is "Running" and use the correct host/port/password.
- **How do I stop billing?** Click "Stop" or "Delete" on your instance.

### For Providers

- **How do I list my GPU?** Install the GPUniq agent on your machine and register through the API.
- **How do I set pricing?** Specify hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly rates when registering.
- **How do I get paid?** Earnings accumulate in your balance and can be withdrawn in USDT.
